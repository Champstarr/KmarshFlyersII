Kawainui Combat Flyers (KCF) promotes the recreational hobby and sport of radio controlled model aircraft flying. KCF primairly flies 1/12 scale combat R/C aircraft at the Kawainui Model Flying Field in Kailua, Hawaii on the island of Oahu.

KAILUA WEATHER

Kawainui Combat Flyers (KCF) fly at the Kawainui Model Airfield next to Kailua Town. RC Combat happens on Saturdays (weather permitting) and starts at approximatley 1:00 PM and typically ends around 5:00 PM.

In addition to combat aircraft KCF operate sport fixed-wing and scale RC models as well as drones. Kawainui Model Airfield is a City & County owned public park and is open to the general public and RC flying of all generes. Park hours are from 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM everday of the year.
